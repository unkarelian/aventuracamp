Support

Frequently Asked Questions

What AI providers does Aventura support? expand_more

Aventura supports 70+ LLM providers via OpenRouter, including OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and many more. You can also connect to any OpenAI-compatible API endpoint.

Is my data private? expand_more

Yes. Aventura uses a local-first architecture. Your stories and data stay on your device unless you explicitly choose to sync. We have zero telemetry and no tracking.

Can I use Aventura offline? expand_more

The app runs locally, but AI features require an internet connection to communicate with your chosen AI provider. Grammar checking via Harper.js works entirely offline.

Is Aventura free? expand_more

Yes, Aventura is free and open source under the AGPL-3.0 license. You may need API keys for AI providers, which may have their own costs.

Can I import my SillyTavern characters? expand_more

Yes! Aventura supports importing SillyTavern character cards (V1/V2 JSON and PNG formats) as well as custom lorebooks.
